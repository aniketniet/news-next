About the Event

Don't Miss the Upcoming Event

India International Mine Expo 2026 (IIMX 2026) is set to be the country's premier International Platform bringing together Policymakers, Industry Leaders, Innovators, and Stakeholders from the Mining, Coal, Steel, and Heavy Industries sectors. As India advances toward Self-Reliance, Energy Security, and Sustainable Growth, this landmark event will spotlight Cutting-Edge Technologies, Innovative Solutions, Progressive Policies, and Strategic Partnerships shaping the future of Mining and Allied Industries.

The Expo will offer unparalleled opportunities for Dialogue, Collaboration, and Investment across one of the world's most vital sectors driving global economic progress. Featuring High-Level Conference Sessions, a Dynamic Exhibition, B2B Meetings, an Exhibitor Forum, and Special Inaugural and Valedictory Ceremonies, IIMX 2026 will encompass the entire spectrum of Mines, Minerals, Coal, Steel, Heavy, and Downstream Industries — marking a milestone in charting the future of Sustainable Mining and Industrial Growth.

Why Hyderabad & Telangana?

Mining & Mineral Strengths of Telangana

Coal Powerhouse

Home to Singareni Collieries Company Limited (SCCL), Telangana is one of India's largest coal producers, contributing significantly to the nation's power sector with over 65 million tonnes annually.

Strategic Minerals

Rich deposits of barytes, dolomite, bauxite, uranium, and other rare earth minerals strengthen Telangana's role in resource security and future industrial growth.

Industrial & Business Ecosystem

  • Heavy Industry Hub: Hyderabad is a stronghold for engineering, heavy machinery, and mining equipment manufacturers, making it a natural host city for global collaborations.
  • Innovation & Technology: With world-class research institutions, startups, and global corporations, Hyderabad integrates technology with traditional industries, driving sustainable growth.
  • Logistics & Connectivity: With an international airport, strong rail and road networks, and central geographic location, Hyderabad offers seamless access to domestic and global markets.

Venue – HITEX

Established in 2003, Hyderabad International Trade Expositions Limited, also known as HITEX, was created with the vision of providing a world-class facility for organizing events, exhibitions, conferences, live shows and trade fairs. Located in the heart of Hyderabad, India, HITEX is a premier MICE destination and has hosted 1500+ national and international events and expositions over the years.

📍

Venue – HICC

Hyderabad International Convention Centre (HICC) is managed by Accor, one of the world's leading hotel operators, and is jointly owned by Emaar Properties PJSC Dubai and Telangana State Industrial Infrastructure Corporation Ltd. HICC is a purpose-built, state-of-the-art convention facility, the first-of-its-kind and the best standalone convention centre in South Asia.
